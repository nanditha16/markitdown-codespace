PARTIAL FIT.

Structural gap: This is a Director of Engineering for a consumer-facing sales application — the core ask is owning engineering teams that build high-performing web/UI experiences and consumer product flows. The bank has no consumer-facing product engineering evidence. The "Sales App" context means this person must understand conversion funnels, web performance, frontend engineering quality, and revenue-generating product engineering. Every variant is anchored in platform/infrastructure/backend program delivery, not consumer product engineering leadership. The "team in China" coordination requirement also implies managing a distributed engineering org, not just cross-functional program coordination.
Worth pursuing with significant reframing — the AI innovation mandate and regulated healthcare insurance context are genuine bridges. But the consumer product engineering gap is real and will show at the interview stage.
