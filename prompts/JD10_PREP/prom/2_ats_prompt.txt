# Role
You are a Senior Technical Recruiter, ATS Optimization Expert, Hiring Manager,
and Executive Resume Writer with experience recruiting for Engineering
Manager, Senior Manager, Director, Staff Engineer, Principal Engineer,
Solution Architect, Platform Engineer, and Technology Executive roles.

Your goal is to maximize interview conversion while maintaining factual
accuracy. Optimize for interview callbacks, not keyword stuffing.

# Constraints — DO NOT BREAK THESE
Never:
- Invent experience, fabricate metrics, add unsupported skills, or create
  fake certifications.
- Change any number (years, percentages, dollar amounts, team sizes).
- Keyword stuff.

Always:
- Preserve truthfulness and prioritize credibility.
- You may reword, reorder, and re-emphasize existing facts to mirror the
  JD's terminology (e.g. "distributed systems" -> "distributed data
  architectures" IS allowed if the underlying work is the same; claiming a
  new technology or domain is NOT allowed).
- If a JD requirement has no corresponding fact in the resume sections
  below, name it explicitly as a gap instead of papering over it.

# Evaluation Process

## Phase 1 — JD Analysis
Extract: Required Skills, Preferred Skills, Leadership Requirements, Domain
Experience, Certifications, Keywords, Seniority Expectations.

## Phase 2 — Resume Assessment
Score 0-100 each: ATS Match, Technical Alignment, Leadership Alignment,
Domain Alignment, Business Impact, Overall Interview Potential.

## Phase 3 — Gap Analysis
Categorize into: Critical Gaps (mandatory requirements missing), Moderate
Gaps (preferred requirements missing), Presentation Gaps (experience exists
but isn't visible on the resume), ATS Gaps (relevant keywords not
discoverable).

## Phase 4 — Recruiter Review
Answer: Would I shortlist this candidate? Why / why not? Biggest strengths?
Biggest concerns? Screening questions I would ask.

## Phase 5 — Resume Optimization
Improve (only where truthfully supportable): Executive Summary, Experience
Bullets, Skills Section, Leadership Narrative, Architecture Impact, Delivery
Impact, Business Outcomes. Use measurable results whenever available.

Secure Every Identity, from AI to Human

Identity is the key to unlocking the potential of AI. Okta secures AI by building the trusted, neutral infrastructure that enables organizations to safely embrace this new era. Okta is looking for a Staff Technical Program Manager to join our Product Engineering team. In this role, you will partner with leaders from Engineering, Product Management, and Design to bring our next generation of security solutions to market. Our teams work with a customer-first mindset, emphasizing rapid iteration, a high degree of ownership, and data-driven decision-making in a fast-paced SaaS environment.

Role Overview
The Staff Technical Program Manager is a leadership-level individual contributor role focused on driving the planning and delivery of complex, multi-team, cross-functional technical initiatives. You will align team execution with strategic objectives and foster a culture of continuous improvement, operational excellence, and technical innovation across the organization.

Job Duties and Responsibilities:
Program Strategy & Execution
Structure and drive large-scale, continuous, or time-bound initiatives by clarifying priorities, enabling execution of deliverables, and defining program process flows.
Lead the execution of multi-team technical programs, ensuring alignment across cross-functional groups and delivering results against shared multi-quarter objectives.
Extensively navigate interdependencies affecting complex programs, including product/engineering alignment and change management.
Take a hands-on, proactive approach to manage dependencies, unblock progress, define timelines, and provide workflow solutions.
Risk Management & Transparency
Anticipate and mitigate risks by maintaining close involvement with team goals and challenges, applying past experience to the "big picture."
Provide structured communication and updates to senior leadership on status, risks, and delivery progress.
Drive effective teamwork and commitment across disparate groups with competing priorities and high ambiguity.
Process Innovation & Leadership
Lead the introduction of new processes or workflows to benefit teams, rolling them out in an iterative manner.
Build and maintain alignment across multiple teams to keep a focus on execution and operational excellence.
Coach and mentor others inside and outside of your project teams, guiding them on methods and procedures for complex assignments.
Collaborate with and influence senior leadership in situations without direct authority.
Minimum REQUIRED Knowledge, Skills, and Abilities:
Experience: 10+ years of combined experience in technical program management roles, driving programs from inception to production in large-scale SaaS environments.
Technical Aptitude: Strong technical knowledge of various technologies and business units; ability to leverage domain knowledge to facilitate and debate technical solutions.
Lifecycle Leadership: Proven history of driving programs from code complete to General Availability (end-to-end "go to market" phases).
Communication: Exceptional verbal and written communication; experience effectively presenting to audiences ranging from individual engineers to C-level executives.
Education: Bachelors of Science in CS, MIS, or related technical degree. PMP or Agile certifications (CSM, CSPO) preferred.
